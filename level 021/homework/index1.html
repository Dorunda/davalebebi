Mercedes W124

he Mercedes-Benz W124 is a range of executive cars made by Daimler-Benz from 1984 to 1997.
The range included numerous body configurations, and though collectively referred to as the W-124,
internal chassis designations varied by body style: saloon (W 124); estate (S 124); coupé (C 124);
(A 124); limousine (V 124); rolling chassis (F 124); and long-wheelbase rolling chassis (VF 124).

მეორე სტატია W124

From 1993, the 124 series was officially marketed as the E-Class. The W 124 followed the 123
series from 1984 and was succeeded by the W 210 E-Class
(saloons, estates, rolling chassis) after 1995, and the C 208 CLK-Class (coupés, and cabriolets) in 1997.
