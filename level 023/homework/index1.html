The W212 and S212 Mercedes-Benz E-Class series is the fourth generation of the E-Class
of executive cars which was produced by Mercedes-Benz between 2009 and 2016 as the successor to the W211 E-Class.
The body styles of the range are either four-door sedan/saloon (W212) or a five-door estate/wagon (S212).
