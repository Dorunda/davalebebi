The sixth generation of the BMW 3 Series consists of the BMW F30 (sedan version),
BMW F31 (wagon version, marketed as 'Touring') and BMW F34
(fastback version, marketed as 'Gran Turismo') compact executive cars.
F30/F31/F34 generation was produced from October 2011 to 2019 and is often collectively referred to as the F30.
